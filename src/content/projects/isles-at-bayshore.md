<div class="section-body-col">

<p>2. Improve user experience, especially on mobile.</p>

<h3 class="section-title">Lo-fi Wireframes</h3>
</div>
<div class="section-intro-col">
<p class="section-intro">Mapping out a visual conceptual blueprint.</p>
</div>
<div class="section-body-col">
<p>To show my vision for the redesign of the content to the rest of the team, I created some basic low-fi sketches with content structure and page layouts.</p>
<p>While going over the proposed layout we talked about incorporating a sign-up form on each page. We worked under the assumption that this could help us with our lead capture. During the implementation process, we tested the two versions (with and without the sign-up form) and decided to keep the form given that it was indeed increasing our lead capture.</p>

<section class="case-study-section">
<div class="section-header-col">
<h3 class="section-title">Overview of Solutions</h3>
</div>
<div class="section-intro-col">
<p class="section-intro">Multi-channel optimization for seamless experience.</p>
</div>
<div class="section-body-col">
<ul>
<li><strong>Website Revamp:</strong> The website needed to be updated to fit the needs of potential clients. The revamp not only focused on better visuals, but also on enabling direct communication with the user as fast as possible.</li>
<li><strong>Email Marketing:</strong> Email is one of the highest converting channels throughout the real estate industry, so email captures and marketing campaigns were implemented to convert more subscribers to customers and increase returns.</li>
<li><strong>Website Speed:</strong> The website loading speed was over 4 seconds. The development team was able to cut it down to less than 1 second. This was possible by reducing the number of images and content displayed on the new design I proposed.</li>
<li><strong>Engaging Content:</strong> We decided to create a dedicated section with relevant content about the industry to rank higher on Google searches. Our copywriter wrote the articles, and they were all original content.</li>
<li><strong>Customer Service:</strong> Our consultant team worked on decreasing the response waiting time on Chatlio from an average of 1:30min to 25seconds.</li>
</ul>
